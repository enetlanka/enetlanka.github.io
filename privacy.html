eNet Lanka Privacy Policy

Dear User, 
We have created this E NET LANKA privacy policy to demonstrate our commitment to your privacy and the protection of your information.

This privacy statement describes what we do with the information that we receive via our online forms in the www.enetlanka.com web site. 

There are three areas where we gather user data in this the web site. The Online Contact Us form, The Subscription form and The Unsubscription Form.

Any time you send any information via our forms, you will receive an e-mail from the web site confirming your action (it might be a subscription, unsubscription or an inquiry). 

The Online Contact Us form sends our support team an e-mail with the data that you have entered. The Subscription Form captures the data and saves it in our E-mail Database which is not accessible to any other party but E NET LANKA. The Unsubscription Form Captures your E-mail Address and saves it in our Unsubscription Database.

If you visit our site to browse, or to read or download information (ALL VISITORS)

We automatically receive information about the country, computer that you are using, including IP address, browser software, operating system, and the name of the domain and host from which you access the Internet. We also record the date and time you access our site and the Internet address of the Web site from which you linked directly to our site. 

Why did you receive an email from us?

If you received a mailing from us,
(a)    your email address is listed as being a subscriber in our or our one or more account holders' mailing list

(b)    your email address is listed as being an account holder or

(c)    You have an existing business relationship with our account holder and have expressly shared this address for the purpose of receiving information in the future ("opt-in").


If You Subscribe

We receive your name, email address, age, gender information. Later on we might ask you to join in our surveys and proved us more information if you wish to.

We use your email address to deliver you our email advertisements regarding various products, services, business, etc.

Although E NET LANKA E-mail Advertisements may contain occasional links or references to various commercial partners, these links and references do not mean that we rent, sell or otherwise disseminate to commercial partners information that we receive about you. 

How You Can Unsubscribe 

If you no longer wish to receive our newsletter and promotional communications, you may opt-out of receiving them by visiting our website and entering your e-mail address in the unsubscribe form or the click unsubscribe link provided in all e-mails. It will take not more than 7 days to process your opt-out request.

Account Termination or Unsubscription

Upon termination of your account or unsubscription from our mail lists, both your account data and subscriber data will be deleted or deactivated.
Security and storage of your personal information
We use security measures such as encryption to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of data used by our system.
The Internet is not a 100% secure medium for communication and, accordingly, E Net Lanka cannot guarantee the security of any information you send to E Net Lanka via the Internet. E Net Lanka is not responsible for any loss or damage you or others may suffer as a result of the loss of confidentiality of such information.
E Net Lanka is committed to protecting the security of your personal information. We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. For example, we store the personal information you provide on computer systems with limited access, which are located in controlled facilities.

Sharing and Usage of Account Information

We will never share, sell, or rent your personal account information or subscriber data with anyone without your advance permission or unless ordered by a court of law. Information submitted to us is only available to employees managing this information for purposes of contacting you or sending your emails based on your request for information and to contracted service providers for purposes of providing services relating to our communications with you.

Publish Information 

We do not publish any information that has explicit content, related to any religious back grounds, related to any political background or that harms a human in any way.

Web Site Information Security

This site has used high security mythologies in order to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. In order to prevent unauthorized access to your information, we have instituted safeguards such as physical access restrictions, Network level restrictions, and software defenses on the confidentiality of your personal information. We are also notified in the event of loss of Internet connectivity. However, unauthorized access, loss of connectivity or hardware failure might still occur. For this reason, we cannot guarantee that our precautions are 100% effective at all time. Nor can we be responsible for lost or delayed requests, e-mails, or content that may result from any technical failure.

Injunction Clause

If, for any reason, this policy is violated, you may obtain a legal injunction to prevent the exposure of your data.
Liability Limitation
In no case whatsoever will liability of the Company exceed the amount of monies paid for services nor with the Company be liable for indirect, special, or consequential damages of any kind to any party.
Privacy Policy Changes
If this privacy policy changes in the future, all account holders will be notified of the change at least ten (10) days before it occurs and have the option to terminate his or her account and thus have their data removed from the system.
